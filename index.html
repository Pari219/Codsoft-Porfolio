about

A bit about how I work

I'm a final-year Computer Science & Engineering student at NSUT Delhi, specializing in Data Science. I like building things end-to-end — designing the database, writing the API, shaping the interface, and then asking what the data flowing through it can actually tell us.

My work sits at the intersection of full-stack development and applied machine learning: e-commerce platforms with real authentication and order flows on one side, and CNNs, fuzzy-logic hybrids, and BI dashboards on the other. I've interned in web development at CodSoft and in cybersecurity at C-DAC Noida, which shaped how I think about building software that is both usable and secure.

Outside of coursework, I'm steadily working through data structures and algorithms, and I enjoy the process of taking a rough idea — a dataset, a UI sketch, a research question — and getting it to something that runs.
